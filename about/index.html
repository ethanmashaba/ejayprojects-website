About EJay Projects

A 100% black-owned multidisciplinary engineering, infrastructure and industrial support company built around responsive execution, safety and long-term client value.

Company Background

Built for execution. Structured for growth.

EJay Projects was established in 2016 and has evolved into a diversified industrial support partner capable of supporting mining, commercial, power generation and high-risk operational environments.

Our operating model is practical, responsive and execution-focused, combining technical expertise, safety discipline and reliable project delivery.

Mission

To deliver responsive, compliant and cost-effective engineering, maintenance and operational support solutions that enhance reliability and create sustainable value.

Commitment

We maintain high standards of safety, quality, environmental responsibility and operational performance across projects and support services.

Approach

We drive practical innovation and operational excellence across every project, ensuring quality, efficiency and lasting impact.
